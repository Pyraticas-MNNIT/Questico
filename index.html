General FAQS.
+

Why choose QOKO Events?

QOKO Events is a well established event production company. We have worked on 1000’s of weddings, corporate events & festivals for over 18 years & we believe our knowledge, experience &reputation sets us apart from other hire companies in Sussex. 

+

How do I get a quote for my event?

Really simple, just email or call us using the contact form & give us as much information as possible about your wedding / event or production ideas. We will come back normally the same day with a quote for your event. 

+

Where are you based?

QOKO Events is located on the Faygate Business Centre on Faygate Lane in Horsham. We work all over the UK and work regularly in the Sussex, Surrey & Kent areas.

+

Do you deliver the equipment?

Yes, QOKO Events does not offer the option to pick up & self hire any equipment, we are a turnkey event production company & all of our services are based on us delivering, setting up & collecting the equipment hired after the event. If technical crew are required for your event they will also be provided by us. 

+

How much do you charge for a production?

As you can imagine this highly depends on what you are looking to hire & the scare of the production. If you were to hire a dance floor for your wedding with love letters in the Sussex area, this would typically cost under £1,000 while a marquee with a sound, stage & lighting package can easily cost north of £12,000.
