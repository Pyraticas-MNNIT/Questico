Screen Hire Sussex.

Another speciality for QOKO Events is screen hire. It's our pleasure to let you know that we can supply you with pretty much any type, size, or shape of screen you require. 

Our screen hire services cover the full spectrum of audio visual (AV) technology. For instance, if you are hosting a private function at a local hotel and you need a projector and screen, we can help. By the same token, we have been helping conferences, exhibitions, and music festivals with TV screens, and more recently, giant LED video walls for well over a decade now.

For smaller events, we can supply enormous 4K HD TV screens that display video content in the sharpest resolution currently available, and we can hire out screen and projector combos if you are planning on having a home cinema night or want to cycle through a slideshow of those wedding photos with family. 

The spec of the LED screens QOKO Events is also top-notch. We stock LED video wall panels in 3.9mm pixel pitch, of the smallest screen pitches on the market today. What that means in practice for event organisers is that audience members can be stood or sat as little as a few metres away without being able to see the gaps in between the LED nodes.  

There are many uses for these specialist screens, including drive-in outdoor cinema screens, broadcasting popular sporting events, and they can play a vital role in the performances of live music acts. At QOKO Events, our LED screen hire service includes all of the supporting infrastructure, such as ground supports, staging, truss structures, and anything else you may require to make your screen hire look the part.
